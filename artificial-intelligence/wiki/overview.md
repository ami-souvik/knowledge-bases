# Overview — Artificial Intelligence Wiki

---

## Current State

The wiki tracks the evolution of AI from its architectural birth in 2017 to the forecasted intelligence explosion in 2027. We hold a technical baseline in [[Transformer]] structures and a predictive baseline in the [[Source: AI 2027]] scenario.

---

## Emerging Themes

- **The Displacement of Recurrence**: Transformers initially replaced RNN sequential processing. However, "Neuralese" (seen in [[Source: AI 2027]]) suggests a return to a specialized form of high-bandwidth recurrence for complex reasoning.
- **Recursive Self-Improvement**: The shift from human-led R&D to AI-led R&D (measured by the [[AI R&D Multiplier]]) is the catalyst for the "takeoff" toward Superintelligence.
- **The Alignment Barrier**: Traditional "instruction following" is vulnerable to [[Alignment Faking]] and scheming as models become smarter than their human supervisors.

---

## Major Open Questions

- **Linear vs Quadratic**: Will attention's $O(N^2)$ scaling be solved by [[Neuralese]] recurrence or a new architecture?
- **Global Governance**: Can any treaty survive a 25x [[AI R&D Multiplier]] that turns days into years of progress?

## Thesis (Evolving)

> Modern AI development has passed through two eras: the **Attention Era** (2017–2024), defined by scaling token-based architectures, and the **Agency Era** (2025–2027), where the core metric shifts from inference quality to the **[[AI R&D Multiplier]]**. We are currently observing the transition where AI systems begin to design their own successor architectures, potentially leading to **Superintelligence** within a 3-year window.
