# Google Brain

**Kind:** Company / Research Lab  
**Affiliation / Creator:** Google

## Overview
Google Brain was an elite deep learning research team within Google. It was responsible for many foundational breakthroughs in AI, most notably the development of the [[Transformer]] architecture. It has since been merged with DeepMind to form Google DeepMind.

## Key Contributions / Capabilities
- **Development of the Transformer**: Introduced in the 2017 paper [[Source: Attention Is All You Need]].
- **TensorFlow**: The primary library that powered the first wave of deep learning.
- **Distilling Knowledge**: Pioneer in model compression and knowledge distillation.
