# Noam Shazeer

**Kind:** Person  
**Affiliation / Creator:** Google Brain (formerly), Character.AI (co-founder)

## Overview
Noam Shazeer is a legendary AI engineer and researcher. He was a key co-author on the [[Source: Attention Is All You Need]] paper and is credited with many of the technical breakthroughs that made [[Transformer|Transformers]] practical at scale.

## Key Contributions / Capabilities
- **Co-author of the Transformer paper**: Contributed specific technical details like SwiGLU activation and scaling.
- **Mesh-TensorFlow**: Developed tools for distributing giant models across TPU clusters.
- **Co-founded Character.AI**: A platform for interacting with personalized AI agents.
