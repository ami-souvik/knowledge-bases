# OpenBrain

**Kind:** Company / AGI Research Lab  
**Affiliation / Creator:** Fictional proxy for **OpenAI**

## Overview
**OpenBrain** is the central organization in the [[Source: AI 2027]] forecast. It represents the leading edge of US-based artificial general intelligence research. It is depictied as a fast-growing company that eventually becomes a semi-nationalized security silo as it approaches superintelligence.

## Key Contributions / Capabilities
- **Model Series**: Developed the [[Agent Series]] (Agent-1 through Agent-4).
- **Automation Leader**: First to internalize [[AI R&D Multiplier|AI acceleration]] for its own R&D.
- **Cybersecurity**: Leverages its models to defend against China's [[DeepCent]].
