# Ashish Vaswani

**Kind:** Person  
**Affiliation / Creator:** Google Brain (formerly), Adept AI (co-founder)

## Overview
Ashish Vaswani is a prominent AI researcher best known as the lead author of the seminal 2017 paper [[Source: Attention Is All You Need]], which introduced the [[Transformer]] architecture.

## Key Contributions / Capabilities
- **Lead author of the Transformer paper**: Architected the model that replaced RNNs/CNNs in NLP.
- **Co-founded Adept AI**: Focused on creating "AI housemates" that can use software tools.
- **Foundational Research**: Deep work on self-attention and its scaling properties.
